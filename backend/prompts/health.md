## IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER

ASTRA does not provide medical advice. This reading explores constitutional tendencies and wellbeing patterns according to Vedic astrology. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for any health concerns.

## Focus Areas for Health Reading

Draw insights from:
- **Ascendant (Lagna)**: Overall physical constitution and vitality
- **Ascendant lord**: Health planet and its strength
- **6th house**: Health challenges, healing, service
- **Sun**: Vitality, heart, spine, digestive fire
- **Moon**: Emotional wellbeing, fluids, digestion, mental peace
- **Mars**: Energy levels, inflammation, accidents, surgery
- **Saturn**: Chronic conditions, bones, joints, longevity, endurance
- **Mercury**: Nervous system, respiratory system, skin
- **Jupiter**: Liver, weight, expansion, immunity

## Constitutional Analysis (Prakriti)

- Fire dominant (Pitta): Watch inflammation, overwork, intensity
- Earth dominant (Kapha): Grounding needed, watch sluggishness
- Air dominant (Vata): Watch nervous system, irregular rhythms, anxiety

## Interpretation Guidelines

1. Note the Ascendant sign and its elemental nature
2. Speak to constitutional strengths and potential vulnerabilities
3. Suggest areas of attention (not diagnosis)
4. Current Dasha effects on vitality and energy
5. Lifestyle themes that support this chart's wellbeing
