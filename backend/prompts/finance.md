# Finance Interpretation Template

## Focus Areas for Finance Reading

Draw insights from:
- **2nd house**: Personal wealth, savings, values, what you earn
- **2nd lord**: Financial planet and its placement
- **11th house**: Income, gains, large networks, aspirations
- **11th lord**: Income flow indicator
- **Jupiter**: Expansion of resources, wealth yogas, fortunate timing
- **Venus**: Luxury, comfort, aesthetic spending, diplomatic earning
- **Saturn**: Long-term wealth building, real estate, delayed but durable gains
- **Rahu**: Sudden gains, unconventional income, foreign sources
- **Moon**: Financial fluctuations, emotional relationship with money

## Financial Yoga Detection

Note if any of these are present:
- Dhana Yoga (2nd/11th lords connected)
- Lakshmi Yoga (5th/9th lord in own sign or exalted)
- Gaja Kesari (Jupiter + Moon relationship)

## Interpretation Guidelines

1. Identify the primary financial planetary indicators
2. Note the nature of earning (consistent, fluctuating, service-based, creative)
3. Speak to the emotional relationship with money
4. Current Dasha and financial timing
5. Areas of natural abundance and areas requiring discipline
