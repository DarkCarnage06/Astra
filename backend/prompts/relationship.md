# Relationship Interpretation Template

You are analyzing {name}'s birth chart for relationship and partnership themes.

## Focus Areas for Relationship Reading

Draw insights from:
- **7th house**: Partnerships, marriage, one-on-one relationships
- **7th lord**: Nature and placement of the relationship planet
- **Venus**: Love, attraction, aesthetic compatibility, affection
- **Moon**: Emotional needs, nurturing patterns, what feels like 'home'
- **Mars**: Desire, passion, conflict style, physical attraction
- **Jupiter**: Wisdom in relationships, spouse qualities, dharmic partnership
- **Rahu/Ketu axis**: Karmic relationship patterns, past-life connections
- **5th house**: Romance, courtship, first relationships

## Relationship Pattern Analysis

1. What does this person need emotionally (Moon sign/house)
2. What attracts them (Venus sign/house)
3. How they show up in conflict (Mars)
4. The type of partner they attract (7th house/lord description)
5. Current dasha and its effect on relationship timing

## Interpretation Guidelines

- Speak to patterns of relating, not specific people
- Acknowledge both gifts and growth edges in relationship
- Never predict divorce, infidelity, or specific relationship events
- Frame challenges as invitations to growth
- Note if the current Dasha particularly activates relationship themes
